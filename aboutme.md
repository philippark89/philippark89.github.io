## My name is Taeju Park

I am a Platform and DevOps engineer with 4 years of experience automating cloud infrastructure and delivery pipelines, primarily on AWS. I specialize in infrastructure-as-code with Terraform, CI/CD automation, and building reliable, scalable container-based deployments.

### My interests

Outside of work, I'm into homelabbing — self-hosting my own services, mostly for home automation. I also enjoy playing games with friends, which often turns into building small third-party apps to make things more convenient: Discord bots that pull game records from official APIs, or little tools and macros to smooth out everyday tasks. Most of these run on my self-hosted homelab server, so my hobbies tend to feed right back into it.

### My story

It started with games. I loved playing them, but I was always just as curious about how they were built — how something so fun and complex actually came together under the hood. That curiosity pulled me into programming: I started learning languages and building things, drawn to the idea that I could create something that genuinely helped people.

Like a lot of software engineers, I dreamed of building my own game. Along the way I ended up building plenty of game utility apps for players instead — and rather than just shipping them, I wanted to run the services myself. I set up an on-prem server in my homelab to host them, and keeping those services alive is what pulled me into infrastructure. What started as a way to support my own apps turned into the work I do today: building reliable, automated platforms.
